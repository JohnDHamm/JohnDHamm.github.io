Multi-athlete lap timer

Team Timer is an app developed from necessity. As a coach for a youth triathlon team, I and the other coaches struggled with a way to record athletes' workouts during a training session. Very often we run a workout where all the athletes of a particlar training group start together and complete the same number of laps. Despite utilizing multiple stopwatches, clipboards, and other methods, recording each athlete's lap time, while also trying to convey to each athlete their progress, became frustratingly difficult. Thus, Team Timer was born!

Team Timer is built around a team/group/coach dynamic. Coaches can create a new team or join one already registered by another coach. When athletes are added to a team by a coach, they are assigned to a specific workout group.

Once logged in, each coach has their own "locker" where they can choose to edit their team's groups or athletes, view results from workouts they have previously recorded, or start a new workout.

After entering all the needed information for the workout, and selecting which athletes will be participating, coaches are automatically taken to the timer...

The workout starts with a press of the timer start button at the top, where the overall time and the number of laps currently completed by all athletes is displayed. Athletes' individual timing buttons are listed in order of the the athletes' average speed to help the coach predict the next lap finisher. As each athlete button is pressed, their current lap time and lap count is recorded and displayed for the coach to let the athlete know how they are progressing.

After the last athlete has completed the last lap, coaches are immediately taken to the workout view so they can share the results with the athletes, including each lap time, overall time, and average pace per lap.

Animation

In 1998, I embarked on a mission to turn a hobby into a new career that has spanned more than 17 years. After starting out as an independent traditional (2D) animator, I eventually entered the realm of 3D computer animation and worked as an animation director and production manager.

  • Tennessee Titans scoreboard
    Working with Kroger, Shoe Carnival, and the Tennessee Titans, I created fun contests for one of the largest screens in the NFL to engage and entertain the fans during timeouts.
    Concept/Writing/Design/Direction
  • Elmo's Alphabet Adventure home video
    Created for Sesame Workshop, I oversaw a team of around 20 artists to produce over 40 minutes of animation in many different styles for a home video.
    Animation Direction/Design
    Teams of CG artists worked hard and had fun recreating the look and feel of popular video games.
    The designers and animators met the challenge of working in different styles and animation mediums.
    Our designers had fun creating the outfits for this section, while our assembly artists and compositors nailed the lighting and atmosphere of this rockin’ contest. (Although I still wish the budget had allowed me to add the lasers!)
    Designing characters and props to be recognizable within the restrictions of using only a limited amount of squares was tricky.
    Utilizing state-of-the-art motion capture technology, I had the joy of directing a very talented, trained dancer to play Elmo and a “not-so-trained, but still quite talented in his own way” animator to “dance” the part of the elf.
    16 different CG versions of the 4 main characters had to be designed, modeled, rigged and animated, along with 6 different sets, including this large world built for an exciting race.
    As many as 17 different animators were needed during production, creating amazing performances while keeping to an ambitious timeline.

  • Coca-Cola VFS Training
    Leading a team of Coca-Cola safety experts, spanning across 12 time zones, proved to be a challenging, yet rewarding experience. To produce quality, educational equipment training videos, as well as animations depicting actual factory accidents, the safety experts who had no prior experience with video or animation production had to be educated and guided through the process. Acting as both project manager and director on the production provided a single point of contact for information, organization and feedback.
    Concept/Writing/Design/Direction/Producing
  • Huge IO video open
    An animated opening for training videos that in itself demonstrates a principle of the company's teaching. Combines a 3D element with traditional 2D character animation.
    Design/Direction/Animation
  • CDC Flu Shots commercial
    An animated P.S.A. using a combination of 2D and 3D animation with visual fx to help educate the general public of the dangers of the flu and how to help protect themselves and their families
    Direction/Animation/Compositing
  • Dave Mason Auto commercial
    A series of humorous animated car commercials for a small dealership in Indiana.
    Concept/Writing/Design/Direction/Animation
  • Columbus Blue Jackets game open
    Created to pump up the fans at the start of the game, this animation plays on the jumbotron at the NHL team's home games.
    Concept/Design/Direction
  • Brad Paisley's Muddi Gras show open
    Working with C.M.T., the famous country artist was brought to animated life for the opening to his concert T.V. special.
    2D Character Animation
  • Fiancé Visa P.S.A. commercial
    Created as a public service announcement for the Armed Forces Network, this commercial was produced in the style of a popular 1960's sitcom animated show open.
    Design/Animation/Direction/Compositing
